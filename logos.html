Creative Direction · Brand Identity 2025

FinExperia Brand Marks

Four distinct concepts. Each tells the Finance + Experience story a different way.

Design principle: Every mark has a visual "aha!" — a second read that rewards attention.
Dark Background
01Trade

The letter "F" is a bullish candlestick chart. Candle 1 (peach, narrow) = learning phase. Candle 2 (coral, wider + taller) = execution and growth. Finance students read the chart first, then see the brand initial.

Brand identity · Website header · App icon
02Signal

EKG flatline → dramatic spike → rising stock chart. One unbroken path, three chapters: dormant theory / the FinExperia jolt / ascending career. The flatline fades in; the glow dot at the end is where you're headed.

Social media · Banners · Merchandise
03Axis

Financial coordinate axes (x/y with arrowheads) + a rising performance curve. Data nodes grow larger toward the peak. Bloomberg terminal precision. Measurement as a form of ambition.

Investor decks · Reports · Certificates
04Cluster

Growing node constellation — each node larger than the last, rising diagonally. The career network you build: more experience = larger node. Radiating rings around the primary node show expanding influence.

Social profiles · Program collateral · Merchandise
Light Background
01Trade

Dark icon preserves contrast on light surfaces. Wordmark shifts to deeper coral tones for readability.

Letterhead · Proposals · White papers
02Signal

Dark icon box maintains the EKG story on any background. Full-print compatible.

Course materials · Brochures · Print
03Axis

Self-contained on dark base. Ideal for charts, infographics, and financial reports where data-precision aesthetics matter.

Annual reports · Pitch decks · Data sheets
04Cluster

Deeper coral on cream for maximum contrast. Node network reads clearly on all print applications.

Certificates · Merchandise · Social banners
